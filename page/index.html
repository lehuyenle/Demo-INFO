IS UW ORGANISATIE AL PRIVACY-PROOF?

Per 25 mei 2018 geldt de nieuwe Privacywet. De Wet Bescherming Persoonsgegevens wordt Algemene Verordening Gegevensbescherming (AVG). In de nieuwe wet zijn de regels voor het beschermen van privacy aangescherpt. Voor jouw organisatie betekent dit dat je aan strengere eisen moet voldoen, om de privacy van je klanten te beschermen. Lees hier kort wat de AVG inhoudt. Wil je weten hoe je ervoor zorgt dat jouw praktijk aan alle eisen voldoet? Maak dan gebruik van FG Dichtbij. FG Dichtbij is een slimme doe-privacy-zelf tool, speciaal ontwikkeld voor kleinere organisaties en zorg-praktijken.

YOUR FUNCTIONARY DATA PROTECTION CLOSE

WHAT CAN YOU EXPECT FROM YOUR FG CLOSE?

A Data Protection Officer (FG) is someone who is responsible for the Privacy Policy within an Organization. A healthcare practice usually does not have such an official. For your practice, FG Dichtbij has been made available in consultation with the umbrella organization. FG Dichtbij is a do-it-yourself variant of the Data Protection Officer. With FG Dichtbij you can make your organization compliant. You get clear instructions, there are extensive whitepapers, explanation videos and you prepare your organization yourself by making choices and carrying out activities. In this way, your practice can meet the basic conditions stipulated in the new law. If the result of the questions and answers show that there is an additional need for your practice in the field of Privacy, Then you can implement this directly through our additional services. With FG Dichtbij you can fully focus on your business activities, knowing that you meet the legal requirements.

LEGAL DOCUMENTS

On the legal level there are three things that you need to arrange well. Firstly, you will have to adjust the General Conditions so that they comply with the new rules. Secondly, you will make a Privacy Statement or modify the existing statement. It is also important to make clear agreements about sharing personal data with other organizations. You record these agreements in a Processing Agreement. How we do this we explain per section in an instruction video.
